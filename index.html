Gym Secrets: Trends You Never Knew About!

Hi, everyone! Welcome to our project, where we explore gym check-in patterns and user demographics to uncover key trends in fitness behavior. By offering insights into peak workout times, demographic trends, and popular locations, we aim to help fitness enthusiasts optimize their schedules and support newcomers in starting their fitness journeys with confidence, inspired by the habits of regular gym-goers. For gyms, this project provides valuable data to enhance services, streamline staffing, and create targeted marketing strategies to better meet client needs. Together, we strive to create a more informed, inclusive, and accessible fitness environment for all.
